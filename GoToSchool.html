Key Features

  • Advanced collision detection using the Separating Axis Theorem (SAT) for smooth and precise object interactions.
  • Optimized performance with Quadtree spatial partitioning to maintain high frame rates even in complex scenes.
  • Diverse enemy types with distinct movement behaviors and resistances to challenge your strategy and reflexes.
  • Intuitive, desktop-friendly user interface for a seamless and immersive gameplay experience.
  • Multiple handcrafted maps to encourage creative tactics and enhance replayability.
